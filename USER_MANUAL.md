## 🏠 For Residents

> *No more waiting by the gate wondering if the truck already passed.*

### 📍 Track — See the Truck Coming in Real Time

The moment you open the app, the **Track** tab shows you a live map centered on your home.

- A **1 km circle** is drawn around your address — your personal alert zone
- Trucks on active routes appear as **green pins** on the map
- When a truck enters your 1 km zone, the pin turns **red** and a banner appears at the top of the screen: **"🚛 Truck Nearby!"**
- If you've enabled notifications, you'll get an alert even if the app is in the background

> **Tip:** Tap a truck pin to see its plate number, waste type, and current route — and even send a message directly to the collector.

---

### 📅 Schedule — Know What's Being Collected and When

The **Schedule** tab shows all upcoming and active collection schedules for your area.

Each card tells you:
- 📍 Which areas are being covered
- ♻️ What type of waste is being collected (Biodegradable, Recyclable, etc.)
- 📅 The date and time slot
- 🚛 Which truck is assigned

No more guessing which day to put out which bin.

---

### 📋 Report — Your Voice Matters

Missed a collection? Truck was hours late? The **Report** tab lets you file a complaint directly to the MENRO office.

1. Choose the type: **Missed Collection**, **Delay**, or **Other**
2. Select the route it's about
3. Enter your address and describe what happened
4. Attach a photo as evidence
5. Hit **Submit** — the admin is notified immediately

You can track the status of all your past reports right below the form. When it's resolved, you'll see it update to ✅ **Resolved**.

---

### 👤 Your Profile

Tap the **👤** icon in the top corner to update your name, email, password, or home address. You can re-pin your location on the map anytime — keeping your proximity alerts accurate.

---

### 🔔 Notifications & App Install

- Tap the **🔔 bell** to see your notification history (nearby truck alerts, schedule cancellations)
- Tap **⬇️ Install App** to add EcoTrack to your home screen and use it like a native app

## 🚛 For Collectors

> *Everything you need for your route — in your pocket.*

### 🗺️ Map — Navigate Your Route

When you open the app, a **Route Selection** screen appears. Pick today's schedule and tap **Confirm Route**.

From that moment:
- Your **GPS location is broadcast live** to the server and visible to the admin
- A **turn-by-turn route** is drawn on the map, routing you through your collection areas and nearby residents
- The route **automatically updates** as you move — no manual refresh needed
- **Resident home pins** are shown along your route so you know exactly who to stop for

> The map re-routes every 50 meters of movement, keeping your path always up to date.

---

### 📝 Log Issue — Report Segregation Violations On the Spot

Spotted improperly segregated waste? Don't let it slide — log it in seconds.

1. Enter the address of the violation
2. Select the waste type involved
3. Describe the issue
4. Tap **📷 Take Photo** to capture evidence with your camera right then and there
5. Tap **Submit** — the resident is notified automatically

Your submitted issues are saved and visible to the admin for follow-up.

---

### ✅ Collection — Mark Your Route Complete

Once you've finished a route:

1. Go to the **Collection** tab
2. Select the schedule from the dropdown
3. Tap **✅ Complete Collection** and confirm

The schedule is marked as completed, the route disappears from the map, and the admin sees it update in real time.

---

### 💬 Chat — Stay Connected with Residents and Admin

A floating **💬** button is always on screen. Tap it to open your message inbox.

- Residents can message you directly from the map
- You can reply without leaving the app
- Drag the button anywhere on screen so it never gets in your way
- A badge shows unread message count

## 🛠️ For Admins

> *One dashboard. Every truck, every complaint, every collector — all in one place.*

### 🗺️ Live Map — Your Fleet at a Glance

The Live Map shows every truck currently on a route, updating every 5 seconds.

- Click any truck pin to see its plate, collector name, waste type, and current route
- **⚠️ Orange pins** flag trucks that haven't sent a GPS update recently — so you know immediately if a collector is offline
- Resident home locations are also shown, giving you a full picture of coverage
- Use **💬 Chat** on any truck card to message the collector directly

---

### 🚛 Operations — Manage Everything

The Operations section is your control center, organized into sub-tabs:

#### 📅 Schedules
Create, edit, and delete collection schedules. Assign a truck, set the date, time slot, waste type, and the areas to be covered. Schedules flow through `pending → in-progress → completed` automatically as collectors work their routes.

#### ⚙️ Fleet
Add new trucks, assign collectors to them, and manage their waste type and route assignments. Disable or remove trucks that are out of service.

#### ♻️ Waste Types
Define the waste categories your municipality collects — Biodegradable, Recyclable, Residual, Special Waste, or any custom type. Assign each a color for easy identification on the map.

#### 👥 Users
See every registered user. **Approve** pending resident registrations, **disable** accounts that are no longer active, or remove users entirely.

#### ⚠️ No Signal Report
A dedicated report of trucks that have gone silent — no GPS update beyond the configured threshold. Use this to quickly identify collectors who may need assistance.

#### ⚙️ Settings
- **No Signal Threshold** — How many minutes of silence before a truck is flagged (default: 15 min)
- **Nearby Notification Cooldown** — How often residents can receive proximity alerts for the same truck (default: 10 min)

---

### 📋 Complaints — Hear Your Residents

Every complaint filed by a resident lands here. You can see the type, route, address, description, and any attached photos. Once you've addressed it, mark it as **Resolved** — the resident's report updates automatically.

---

## 💡 Tips & Tricks

- **Residents:** Pin your exact home location during registration for the most accurate proximity alerts.
- **Collectors:** Keep the app open while on route — closing it stops GPS broadcasting.
- **Admins:** Use the No Signal Report before end-of-day to confirm all collectors have completed their routes.
- **Everyone:** Install the app on your home screen (tap **⬇️ Install App**) for the fastest access and offline-ready performance.
